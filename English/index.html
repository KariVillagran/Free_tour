Traveling Tips

  • Do not leave your luggage unattended in public places. Do not accept packages from strangers.
  • Leave a copy of your itinerary with family or friends, so they can contact you in case of emergency.
  • Heed the signs to be made about which sectors and should not drive.
  • Carry traveler's checks or credit card instead of cash whenever possible.
  • Use your common sense. In public, not teach large sums of money or use eye-catching , such as jewelry , necklaces and other similar items.
  • Do not engage in any business transaction with strangers on the street.
  • Teach your children to go to an identifiable employee of the place you are visiting , not a stranger , if you get separated from you. Employees are instructed on how to properly proceed. You can find them faster if they stay in a place with an employee.
  • Become familiar and bring Scored phones may require in an emergency (police , Consulate , bank, credit card contact , ambulances , hospitals, public telephone service for emergency assistance ).
  • If you are in trouble , contact the nearest Chilean embassy.
